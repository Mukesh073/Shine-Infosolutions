What is the difference between a Website Designing Company and a Website Development Company?

While often used interchangeably, these terms refer to two distinct, crucial phases of creating a website. At Shine Infosolutions, we have specialized teams for both, ensuring excellence at every stage.

As a leading Website Designing Company in Gorakhpur, our design team focuses on the 'look and feel' and user experience (UX). Their responsibilities include:

  • Visual Blueprint (UI): Creating the visual layout, color schemes, typography, and branding elements.
  • User Journey (UX): Mapping out how a user will navigate the site to ensure it's intuitive, logical, and easy to use.
  • Prototyping: Building wireframes and mockups so you can see and approve the design before any code is written.

Once the design is finalized, our Website Development Company in Gorakhpur division takes over. Our developers are the architects who bring the design to life. Their work involves:

  • Front-End Development: Writing the HTML, CSS, and JavaScript code that creates the visual elements you see and interact with in your browser.
  • Back-End Development: Building the "engine" of the website—the server, database, and applications that power its features, like user logins, forms, and e-commerce functionality.
  • Ensuring Performance: Optimizing the site for speed, security, and responsiveness across all devices.

A successful website requires the seamless integration of both disciplines. We provide both under one roof to create a final product that is not only beautiful but also powerful and technically sound.
